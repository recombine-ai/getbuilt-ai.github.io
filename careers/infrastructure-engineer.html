Senior Infrastructure
Engineer — AI & ML

Location: London, England

Remote: No

Job Level: Manager/Experienced

Life at Recombine

At Recombine, we believe in the transformative power of AI to revolutionize how businesses operate. We're dedicated to creating industry-leading solutions that leverage AI and machine learning to help our clients optimize their workflows, automate processes, and scale efficiently.

To make this happen, we’re looking for people who are curious, driven, and collaborative—those who thrive in a fast-paced environment and want to be part of a high-growth AI company. We care about our people, our clients, and our mission to make AI accessible and impactful for businesses of all sizes.

Your Mission

Recombine is seeking a highly skilled Senior Infrastructure Engineer to join our Professional Services team. This individual will be responsible for the implementation, upgrading, migration, troubleshooting, and enablement of our AI-driven solutions, ensuring seamless integration with client infrastructure. Your expertise will focus on infrastructure, but you will also act as a project leader and advisor to clients and internal teams.

As a Senior Infrastructure Engineer, you will work closely with our clients and partners to design and deploy AI solutions tailored to their needs, drive adoption, and ensure operational success. Your deep technical knowledge and leadership skills will help guide clients through the complexities of AI and infrastructure integration.

What You’ll Do at Recombine

  • Develop a deep understanding of Recombine's AI platform and infrastructure integration.
  • Engage with clients in both technical pre-sales and post-sales activities to ensure smooth adoption of AI solutions.
  • Design and implement system architectures that integrate Recombine’s AI products with third-party applications and infrastructure, tailored to client needs.
  • Support the sales team with infrastructure guidance during RFI/RFPs and POC stages.
  • Provide ongoing infrastructure support for client implementation teams and internal teams throughout project lifecycles.
  • Deliver on-site and remote training for clients and partners, ensuring they understand best practices and optimization strategies.
  • Document solutions and maintain a repository of best practices.
  • Stay current with new product releases and roadmaps, providing education and feedback to both clients and product teams.
  • Act as a project manager, coordinating resources and ensuring successful delivery in collaboration with Recombine’s internal teams and external partners.

What You’ll Bring to the Team

  • Deep infrastructure expertise: 10+ years of experience in infrastructure roles, with a strong understanding of both on-premises and cloud environments.
  • Technical Skills: Expertise in Linux (file systems, package management, logging, etc.), Kubernetes, Windows Server, SQL Server, Active Directory, Redis, ElasticSearch, and similar technologies.
  • Cloud Experience: Hands-on experience with AWS, Azure, and GCP.
  • Virtualization: Expertise in VMware vSphere, Hyper-V, and other virtualization technologies.
  • Networking and Security: Experience with Palo Alto, Sophos, FortiNet, and Cisco firewall solutions.
  • Remote Desktop Services: Proficiency in supporting RDS, RemoteApp, and Citrix environments (both on-premises and cloud).
  • Project Management: 2+ years of project management experience, with excellent communication and organizational skills.
  • Certifications: Relevant infrastructure certifications (CCNA, JNCP, MCSA, VCP).

How to Apply

If you’re passionate about AI and think you’d be a great fit for the role but don’t meet every single requirement, don’t hesitate to apply. We understand that experience comes in many forms, and we value enthusiasm and passion for learning.

At Recombine, we embrace flexibility in how and where work is done, with hybrid and office-based options available depending on team needs. We evaluate applications on a rolling basis, so don’t wait to apply!

To apply, send us your CV and cover letter to hello@recombine.ai.
