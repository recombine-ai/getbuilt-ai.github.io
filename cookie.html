Cookie Policy Effective Date: January 1, 2025

RCMBN LIMITED (“we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to improve user experience, analyze website performance, and support our marketing efforts. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the site owners.

Cookies can be:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device until they expire or you delete them.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Performance and Analytics: To understand how visitors interact with our website, such as which pages are visited most frequently. We use this information to improve our website and user experience.
  • Marketing: To track user behavior and preferences to deliver targeted advertisements and measure the effectiveness of our campaigns.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the basic functionality of our website and cannot be disabled.

Performance and Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to gather data on how visitors interact with our website. These cookies help us understand user behavior and improve our services. For example, we may collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page

Google Analytics cookies may also collect anonymized data to improve functionality.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your online activity to help us deliver personalized ads and measure campaign performance.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services like Google Analytics, which may place cookies on your device. These third parties may collect and use data according to their own privacy policies.

For more information on how Google uses your data, please visit Google’s Privacy Policy.

5. Cookie Consent

When you visit our website, you will see a pop-up banner asking for your consent to place non-essential cookies on your device. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject all non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

You can change your cookie settings at any time via the pop-up banner or your browser settings.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Check your browser’s help section for instructions.
  • Google Analytics Opt-Out: To prevent Google Analytics from tracking your data, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7. Cookie Duration

The lifespan of cookies varies:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until they expire or are manually deleted.

If you’d like more specific information about the duration of cookies used on our website, please contact us at info@recombine.ai

8. Legal Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) by:

  • Seeking your consent for non-essential cookies.
  • Allowing you to opt-out of cookies at any time.
  • Providing transparency about the types of cookies we use and their purposes.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be revised accordingly.
